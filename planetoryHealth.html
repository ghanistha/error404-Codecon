About Planetary Health

Our system monitors key indicators of Earth's health, providing real-time data and analysis to inform policy decisions and individual actions.

Why Planetary Health Matters

Planetary health is an emerging field that recognizes the interdependence of human health and the health of natural systems. Our planet's ecosystems provide clean air, fresh water, food, and a stable climate - all essential for human civilization to thrive.

However, human activities are pushing Earth systems beyond their stable boundaries. Climate change, biodiversity loss, pollution, and land degradation threaten the stability of our life-support systems.

Our Planetary Health System integrates data from satellites, ground sensors, and research studies to provide a comprehensive view of Earth's health status.
